The Definition of the Derivative

f'(x) = limΔx→0 [f(x + Δx) - f(x)] / Δx

How can we find the exact, instantaneous rate of change at a single moment in time?

🛹 Skateboard Ramp Steepness

How steep is the ramp at any exact point?

Live Calculation (with Δx = 0.001)
[f(0.001) - f(0.0)] / 0.001 ≈ 0.0

At a position of0.0cm, the steepness is approx.0.00.

(Note: A steepness of 0.0 is flat. A value of 1.0 is a steep 45° angle.)
