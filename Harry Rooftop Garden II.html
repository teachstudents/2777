1

Rooftop Assessment & Planning

Evaluate your space and prepare for construction

Before breaking ground, conduct a thorough assessment of your rooftop space. This crucial first step determines the feasibility and design of your garden. Measure dimensions, evaluate structural capacity, and understand environmental conditions like sun exposure and wind patterns.
📋 Assessment Checklist:
  • Measuring tape and notebook
  • Structural engineer consultation
  • Sun/shade mapping throughout the day
  • Wind measurement tools
  • Building permits and regulations check
  • Access route evaluation
  • Utility location identification
2

Waterproofing & Protection

Protect your roof structure from water damage

Install comprehensive waterproofing to protect your roof structure. This includes a waterproof membrane, root barrier, and protective layers. Proper waterproofing prevents costly water damage and ensures your garden's longevity.
🛡️ Waterproofing Materials:
  • EPDM or TPO waterproof membrane
  • Root barrier fabric
  • Protective fleece layer
  • Sealing tape and adhesives
  • Drainage protection mats
  • Edge flashing materials
  • Professional installation tools
3

Drainage System Installation

Ensure proper water management and flow

Install a comprehensive drainage system to prevent water accumulation. This includes drainage aggregate, perforated pipes, and overflow systems. Proper drainage is essential for plant health and structural protection.
🚰 Drainage Components:
  • Drainage gravel/lightweight aggregate
  • Perforated drainage pipes
  • Geotextile filter fabric
  • Overflow drains and outlets
  • Inspection chambers
  • Pipe fittings and connectors
  • Slope creation materials
4

Growing Medium & Soil

Create the perfect foundation for plant growth

Add specialized lightweight growing medium designed for rooftop conditions. The soil mix should provide excellent drainage while retaining adequate moisture and nutrients for healthy plant growth.
🌱 Soil Mix Components:
  • Lightweight potting mix base
  • Compost and organic matter
  • Perlite or vermiculite for drainage
  • Coconut coir for moisture retention
  • Slow-release organic fertilizer
  • pH testing and adjustment materials
  • Soil thermometer
5

Containers & Raised Beds

Install planters and growing structures

Install various containers and raised beds to accommodate different plant types and create visual interest. Choose containers with proper drainage and appropriate sizes for your selected plants.
📦 Container Options:
  • Cedar or treated wood raised beds
  • Large plastic or fiberglass planters
  • Metal containers with rust protection
  • Fabric grow bags for flexibility
  • Drainage gravel and landscape fabric
  • Container feet or risers
  • Plant saucers for water management
6

Irrigation System Setup

Install efficient watering solutions

Install an efficient drip irrigation system with smart timer controls. This ensures consistent watering while conserving water and reducing maintenance time.
💧 Irrigation Components:
  • Drip irrigation tubing and fittings
  • Adjustable drip emitters
  • Smart timer controller with weather sensors
  • Water source connection kit
  • Pressure regulators and filters
  • Micro-sprinklers for larger areas
  • Backup manual watering supplies
7

Wind Protection & Support

Shield plants from harsh rooftop winds

Install windbreaks and plant supports to protect against strong rooftop winds. This creates a more favorable microclimate for plant growth and prevents damage from wind exposure.
🌬️ Wind Protection:
  • Windbreak screens (50% porosity)
  • Sturdy support posts and anchors
  • Plant stakes and flexible ties
  • Trellis systems for climbing plants
  • Temporary protective barriers
  • Heavy-duty zip ties and clamps
  • Weather-resistant materials
8

Plant Selection & Planting

Choose and plant appropriate varieties

Select plants suited for rooftop conditions: wind-resistant, drought-tolerant varieties that thrive in containers. Include a mix of vegetables, herbs, and flowers for a productive and beautiful garden.
🌿 Recommended Plants:
  • Vegetables: Cherry tomatoes, peppers, lettuce, kale
  • Herbs: Basil, rosemary, thyme, oregano, mint
  • Flowers: Marigolds, petunias, nasturtiums
  • Succulents: Sedums, hens and chicks
  • Grasses: Ornamental varieties for texture
  • Quality potting soil and compost
  • Organic fertilizers and plant food
9

Pathways & Accessibility

Create safe access and movement routes

Install safe walkways and access routes throughout the garden. Use non-slip materials and provide adequate lighting for evening maintenance and enjoyment.
🚶 Access Materials:
  • Non-slip composite decking or pavers
  • Natural stone stepping stones
  • Safety handrails and barriers
  • Solar-powered LED pathway lights
  • Weather-resistant storage benches
  • Tool hooks and organization systems
  • Emergency access considerations
10

Final Setup & Maintenance

Complete installation and establish care routines

Complete your rooftop garden with final touches including furniture, storage, and monitoring systems. Establish maintenance routines for watering, fertilizing, and seasonal care.
🔧 Final Components:
  • Weather-resistant garden furniture
  • Lockable tool storage solutions
  • Weather monitoring station
  • Maintenance schedule and calendar
  • Plant identification labels
  • Seasonal protection materials
  • Garden journal for tracking progress
✓

Your Rooftop Garden is Complete!

Enjoy your urban oasis and fresh harvest
