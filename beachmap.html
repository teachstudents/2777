🏖️ Beach Safety Summary

☀️

Sun Safety

  • • Apply SPF 30+ sunscreen every 2 hours and after swimming
  • • Wear UV-protective sunglasses to protect your eyes
  • • Use wide-brimmed hats for face and neck protection
  • • Seek shade during peak sun hours (10am-4pm)
🌊

Water Safety

  • • Never swim alone - always use the buddy system
  • • Stay within designated swimming areas near lifeguards
  • • Check beach warning flags before entering water
  • • Be aware of rip currents and how to escape them
🚨

Emergency Preparedness

  • • Pack a waterproof first aid kit with basic supplies
  • • Keep emergency contacts accessible and phone charged
  • • Carry a safety whistle for attracting attention
  • • Know the location of nearest lifeguard station
🎒

Beach Essentials

  • • Bring plenty of fresh water to stay hydrated
  • • Use beach umbrellas or pop-up tents for shade
  • • Pack extra towels and dry clothes
  • • Bring healthy snacks to maintain energy levels
