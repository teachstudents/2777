6A Module 1: Getting to know each other

Personal information, daily routines, activities | Units 1-3

📖 Unit 1: Growing up

📖 Reading

Personal information texts, age descriptions, growing up stories

growing age tall old
✍️ Writing

Write about yourself (50 words), describe your growth

🗣️ Speaking

Introduce yourself, ask about age and height

📝 Vocabulary

growing, age, year, old, young, tall, short, baby, child, adult

🔤 Grammar

Present simple (I am/You are), possessive adjectives (my/your)

📚 Key Vocabulary
growing /ˈɡroʊ.ɪŋ/ (verb)
Definition: Becoming bigger, taller, or older; developing
Example: "I am growing taller every year."
中文: 成长，生长
age /eɪdʒ/ (noun)
Definition: The number of years someone has lived
Example: "What is your age?"
中文: 年龄
tall /tɔːl/ (adjective)
Definition: Of great or more than average height
Example: "She is tall for her age."
中文: 高的
🔤 Grammar: Present Simple with "Be"
Rule: I am, You are, He/She/It is, We/They are
✅ Correct: I am 12. / She is tall. / They are students.
❌ Common Error: ✗ I is 12. → ✓ I am 12.

PeriodFocusActivitiesHomework

2Grammar (Be verbs)Fill-in-blanks, pair workGrammar worksheet

🎯 Differentiation Strategies
  • Support: Provide sentence frames ("I am ___ years old"), use picture dictionaries
  • Challenge: Ask students to compare family members ("My father is taller than me")
  • Mixed-Ability: Pair stronger students with weaker students for peer support

📝 Mock Question

I _________ 12 years old.

A) is
B) am
C) are
D) be
✅ Answer: B (am)
Explanation: "I" always takes "am" in present simple.
中文: 主语"I"后面总是用"am"。

📖 Unit 2: Daily life

📖 Reading

Daily routine texts, time expressions, schedule descriptions

morning afternoon evening
✍️ Writing

Describe your daily routine (60 words)

🗣️ Speaking

Talk about your daily schedule, ask about routines

📝 Vocabulary

morning, afternoon, evening, night, get up, breakfast, school, homework, sleep, usually

🔤 Grammar

Present simple for routines, frequency adverbs (always/usually/sometimes)

📚 Key Vocabulary
usually /ˈjuː.ʒu.ə.li/ (adverb)
Definition: Most of the time; normally (about 80% frequency)
Example: "I usually get up at 7:00."
中文: 通常，一般
get up /ɡet ʌp/ (phrasal verb)
Definition: To rise from bed after sleeping
Example: "I get up at 6:30 every morning."
中文: 起床
🔤 Grammar: Frequency Adverbs
Frequency Scale: always (100%) → usually (80%) → sometimes (50%) → never (0%)
Position: BEFORE main verbs (I always eat), AFTER be verbs (She is always late)
✅ Correct: I usually go to school by bus.
❌ Error: ✗ I go usually to school. → ✓ I usually go to school.

📝 Mock Question

She _________ to school at 7:30 every morning.

A) go
B) goes
C) going
D) went
✅ Answer: B (goes)
Explanation: Third person singular (she) requires verb + s in present simple.

8A Module 1: My life

Penfriends, work/play, problems | Units 1-3

📖 Unit 1: Penfriends

📖 Reading

Penfriend letters, email correspondence, friendship stories

penfriend letter email
✍️ Writing

Write a letter to penfriend (90 words)

🗣️ Speaking

Discuss hobbies and work, talk about friendships

📝 Vocabulary

penfriend, letter, email, write, receive, hobby, interest, country, language, culture

🔤 Grammar

Present perfect for experience, object clauses (that/if/whether)

📚 Key Vocabulary
penfriend /ˈpen.frend/ (noun)
Definition: A person you write to regularly but have never met
Example: "I have a penfriend from England."
中文: 笔友
receive /rɪˈsiːv/ (verb)
Definition: To get something that someone sends or gives you
Example: "I received a letter from my penfriend."
中文: 收到，接收
🔤 Grammar: Present Perfect for Experience
Structure: have/has + past participle | Use: Experiences without specific time
Time Words: ever (questions), never (negative), for/since
✅ Correct: I have visited Beijing. / She has never written a letter.
❌ Error: ✗ I have visited Beijing last year. → ✓ I visited Beijing last year.
⚠️ Common Errors
  • Error: Using present perfect with specific past time
    Solution: Use timeline visual. Past simple = specific point.
  • Error: Object clause word order ("Do you know where is he?")
    Solution: Teach "Un-Question Switch" explicitly.

📝 Mock Question (ZhongKao Level)

I _________ a penfriend from England for two years.

A) have
B) have had
C) had
D) am having
✅ Answer: B (have had)
Explanation: "For two years" = action from past to present → present perfect.

🗣️ Speaking

Discuss traditions, express opinions on history

📝 Vocabulary

ancient, Greece, myth, tradition, god, goddess, story, hero, legend, civilization

🔤 Grammar

Past perfect review, passive voice consolidation, relative clauses

📚 Key Vocabulary
ancient /ˈeɪn.ʃənt/ (adjective)
Definition: Belonging to the very distant past; very old
Example: "Ancient Greece was the birthplace of democracy."
中文: 古代的，古老的
ZhongKao Frequency: HIGH - Appears in reading 3-4 times/year
myth /mɪθ/ (noun)
Definition: A traditional story about gods, heroes, or the origins of the world
Example: "Greek myths include stories about Zeus and Athena."
中文: 神话
🔤 Grammar: Past Perfect
Structure: had + past participle | Use: Action before another past action
Timeline: Past Perfect → Past Simple → Now
✅ Correct: When I arrived, the movie had already started.
❌ Error: ✗ When I arrived, the movie already started. → ✓ had already started.
